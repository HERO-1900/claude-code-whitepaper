投机预测 Speculative Prediction
AI 响应生成过程中，基于流式 token 预测并并行启动"最可能的下一步"工具调用。AI 完成时，执行结果已就绪。 While AI generates its response, the likely next tool call is predicted and executed in parallel. By the time AI finishes, the result is already ready.
🎯
60–80% 命中率 60–80% Hit Rate
大多数情况预测正确，直接使用缓存的投机结果，节省约 150ms 感知延迟，显著提升交互流畅度。 Predictions are correct most of the time, allowing the cached speculative result to be used directly — saving ~150ms of perceived latency.
↩
优雅回退 Graceful Rollback
预测错误时，投机结果被无损丢弃，重新执行正确路径（约 40ms 额外开销），不影响最终正确性。 When prediction misses, the speculative result is discarded cleanly and the correct path re-executes (~40ms overhead), with no impact on correctness.

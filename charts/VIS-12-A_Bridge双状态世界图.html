💡 通俗理解：Bridge 不是"分布式协调员"，而是"跨办公室的快递员+登记员"。左边的收发员（replBridge/bridgeMessaging）把信交给它，它贴邮票（JWT）、按时刻表（pollConfig）跑到对面信箱（Anthropic API）投递，再把回信带回来——期间不拆包、不仲裁、不合并。源码里真的没有 CRDT。 Plain English: Bridge is not a "distributed coordinator" — it's a cross-office courier + log keeper. The local clerks (replBridge / bridgeMessaging) hand it a letter; it stamps (JWT), runs on schedule (pollConfig) to the mailbox (Anthropic API), delivers, and brings replies back. It never opens, arbitrates, or merges. There is literally no CRDT in the source.

关系本质

Brief 模式是一种最小协作单元：恰好两个成员，一方简报 (Brief)、一方执行回报 (Report)，构成 有方向的信任链。

典型用例

Orchestrator 向 Sub-agent 下发子任务（如"读这5个文件并提炼")，Sub-agent 在 scope 内执行完毕后回传结果。

Relationship Essence

Brief is the minimum collaboration unit: exactly two members — one briefs (Brief) and the other executes/reports (Report) — forming a directional trust chain.

Typical Use Case

Orchestrator briefs a Sub-agent with a subtask (e.g. "read these 5 files and summarize"); Sub-agent executes within scope and returns results.

时序特征

• Brief 同步：调用方 await，阻塞直到 Sub-agent 启动并接受契约
• Report 异步：执行过程可流式回传 status，最终结果按 schema 落盘

权限边界

受简方权限恒为简报方的子集（scoped down），契约里的 scope 字段是硬约束，越权请求会被工具层拒绝。

Timing

• Brief is synchronous: caller awaits, blocks until Sub-agent accepts the contract
• Report is async: status streams back during execution; final results land per schema

Permission Boundary

Briefed permissions are always a subset of Briefing (scoped down). The scope field is a hard constraint — out-of-scope calls are rejected at tool layer.
